Frequently asked questions

Everything to know before purchasing.

Open a question below to review licensing, activation, compatibility, and support details.

Are all features included in every plan?

Yes. The 3-day, 7-day, 14-day, and 30-day licenses include the same automation modules and Telegram controls. Duration and registered-device allowance vary by plan.

How much does each license cost?

The listed prices are $5 or Rp75,000 for 3 days, $8 or Rp120,000 for 7 days, $10 or Rp150,000 for 14 days, and $15 or Rp225,000 for 30 days.

How quickly will I receive my license?

Delivery is handled directly by the seller after payment is confirmed. Contact Telegram, Instagram, or email to confirm current response and delivery times.

How do I activate the license?

Enter the license key when the application asks for it, or place it in the supported configuration file. The application verifies it online before starting.

Is an internet connection required?

Yes. The bot uses strict online validation at startup and while running. It will stop when the license server cannot be reached.

How many devices can use one license?

Device limits depend on the plan: one device for 3 days, two devices for 7 days, three devices for 14 days, and four devices for 30 days. Do not share the key outside the registered devices.

Can I move my license to another computer or VPS?

Contact the seller before moving. A device reset may be required when the existing activation slot is already in use.

Does the license renew automatically?

No. These are one-time purchases for the selected duration. Purchase or request an extension when you want additional time.

Can I extend a license before it expires?

Yes, subject to seller confirmation. Provide your license identifier privately so the remaining period and extension can be checked.

What happens when the license expires?

Online validation is rejected and the bot does not continue running. Your local configuration remains on your device.

Which operating systems are supported?

Use the release made for your operating system and architecture. Available packages may include Windows x64, Linux x64, and Linux ARM64.

Do I need to install Python?

Official compiled releases are intended to run without a separate Python installation. Always follow the instructions included with the downloaded build.

Do I need my own Telegram bot?

Yes. Each user supplies a personal Telegram bot token and chat ID so commands and notifications remain under that user's control.

Do I need to share my game credentials with the seller?

No. Never send passwords, cookies, Telegram tokens, API tokens, or completed environment files. You configure your own credentials locally.

Are updates included?

You may use compatible releases available during the active license period. Review release notes before replacing an existing installation.

What support is included?

Support covers license activation and basic installation guidance. It does not include recovery of game accounts or third-party service credentials.

Can I request a refund?

Ask the seller about the current refund terms before paying. Activated digital licenses may have restrictions, subject to applicable consumer law.

Is using automation risk-free?

No. Automation may violate SimpleMMO rules and can result in restrictions or bans. Every user is responsible for activity performed through their account.

Automation carries account risk.

Review SimpleMMO's rules before use. Automation may result in restrictions or bans, and every user is responsible for activity on their own account.
