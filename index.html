From download to dashboard.

Each user supplies their own SimpleMMO session, Telegram bot, and license key.

Download

Choose the release for Windows or Linux and extract it into a dedicated folder.

Configure

Copy the environment template and add only your own cookies, browser User-Agent, and Telegram credentials.

Activate

Enter your license key. The app verifies it online before any SimpleMMO login or automation starts.

Automation carries account risk.

Review SimpleMMO's rules before use. Automation may result in restrictions or bans, and every user is responsible for activity on their own account.
